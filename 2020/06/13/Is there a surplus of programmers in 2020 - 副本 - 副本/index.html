Is there a surplus of programmers in 2020?

Posted on 2020-06-13 |

There are many such problems.

1, there is no surplus, but it is not so rare. In other words, the Internet is maturing. The demand is more mature and stable.
20,000 Essential Oils Answer: High-end will always be scarce, while low-end will always be redundant.
And then.
The Internet industry is not always good. The development of China’s Internet is closely related to infrastructure, policies, keeping up with the times, etc. Taobao, QQ, which are now giants, did not sit on the bus at the beginning, and it was also very difficult to survive. It’s not too much to go through many wars, so look at the Internet and don’t compare it with big factories. Keep your eyes down.
I am a web worker, so let’s talk about it.
Many years ago, there was no front-end position, but the web became more and more abundant, and this position was gradually separated. at that time, our technology was still three swordsmen, flash, etc. At that time, some of these skills will help you find a job. When Lei Jun was a student, he sold money when he made things himself. But now, it’s not that easy, gadgets, source code, flying everywhere. If it is really lacking, it will be outsourced.
The Internet is slowly developing. Infrastructure has also come up. The business is getting more and more complicated. The front-end development, now you can only three swordsmen, but it is difficult to find a job. (Apart from being proficient in js and the like).
At the front end, the web is also developing, making web pages, and code technology is also developing. making web pages seems less difficult. now buying code, please outsource, can help us to make a project soon, and the cost of building a website is getting lower and lower.
Internet projects are becoming more and more mature and abundant. The problems faced by former grassroots stationmaster and now grassroots stationmaster are also different. In fact, he is also a grass-roots stationmaster, making it more and more difficult to make money. Traffic is getting more and more expensive. It’s getting harder and harder.
What I said may not be relevant to answering the Lord’s questions, but I already answered the Lord’s questions at the beginning. The surplus has not yet arrived. Secondly, there is no shortage of people. Of course, if you are just looking for a job, you can also look at the recruitment, which is still a lot of demand.
Then, quoting Wan essential oil, he replied: In fact, when any industry develops to a certain level, it is scarce at the high end and redundant at the low end.
The reason why I want to say the following is because I want to express my opinion. Because I see a lot of people still indulge in “I’m good at technology, I’m fine, the Internet is still booming” and so on. I feel humble as I am, and I still want to sing the opposite tune.
